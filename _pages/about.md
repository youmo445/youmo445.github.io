Hi, I’m a first-year Ph.D. student at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), majoring in Control Theory and Control Engineering.

My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning (RL), Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, and World Models, with a particular interest in building scalable and generalizable embodied intelligence systems.

I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dongbin Zhao (赵冬斌) and Prof. Haoran Li (李浩然).

Previously, I received my bachelor’s degree from Nanjing University, where I was enrolled in the Industrial Intelligence and Systems Integration Experimental Program at the School of Engineering and Management.

I’m always open to discussions and collaborations related to RL, robotics, and foundation models for decision making.
